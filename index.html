Our End-to-End
Manufacturing Workflow

Sales Inquiry & Quotation

The process starts when a customer shares their requirement or drawing. Our technical and sales team studies the application, specifications, quantity, and delivery expectations

Layout, Estimation & Feasibility

Once the inquiry is received, a preliminary layout and manufacturing plan is created. This stage involves cost estimation, process selection, and feasibility analysis

Work Order Confirmation

After mutual agreement on design, pricing, and delivery schedule, a formal work order is generated. This step confirms project approval and acts as the official go-ahead for execution.

Raw Material Preparation

Detailed production planning is carried out, including raw material selection, cutting layouts, and resource allocation. This ensures optimal material usage, minimal waste

Precision
Machining

Manufacturing operations such as milling and drilling are performed using advanced machines and NC programming. This step focuses on achieving high dimensional accuracy

Inspection & Dispatch

After machining, components undergo thorough quality inspection using measuring instruments and gauges. Once approved, parts are securely packed and dispatched on time
